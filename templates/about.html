About the League

Welcome to the Durban Warhammer 40,000 League! We are a community-driven competitive gaming league dedicated to fostering friendly competition and camaraderie among players of all skill levels.

Our league tracks player ratings across multiple game systems, promotes participation in local stores, and celebrates player achievements through seasonal competitions and rankings.

Scoring System

Players earn points in the following categories:

Category Max Points Description
Generalship 40 Based on game results and win/loss record
Painted 10 Fully painted miniatures (Battle Ready standard)
WYSIWYG 10 What You See Is What You Get - clear models
Games Played 10 Participation bonus - 1 point per game (max 10)
Unique Systems 30 Playing across different game systems

Total Possible: 100 points
